ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence is the science and engineering of making intelligent machines, especially intelligent computer programs. AI is related to task of using computers to understand human intelligence but it doesn’t have to confine itself to methods that are biologically observable.

What is the Position of AI law In India?

There are no specific laws or procedure related to AI in India till the time but there is an initiative by the Government of India to frame guidelines and rules related to AI in India.

Ministry of Industry and Commerce in India, Constituted an 18 members “Task Force on AI for India’s Economic Transformation” comprising of experts, academics, researchers and industry leaders, along with the active participation of governmental bodies and ministries such as NITI Aayog, Ministry of Information Technology (MIT), Department of Science & Technology (DoST), UIDAI and Defence Research and Development Organization in August 2017 and the Task Force submitted the report in March 2018.

Bearing in mind the aspect of AI most countries are quick to form the necessary legislative framework, or advisory councils designed to resolve the issue of regulating AI as a newly formed subject of law.
